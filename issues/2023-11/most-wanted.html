Intro

Since the implementation of the set request feature we have seen over 68,000 set requests made, over 28,500 of which have been fulfilled. Check out what the most wanted games on RetroAchievements are.

DevQuest News

In an effort to help developers accomplish DevQuest 007 we have decided to pick a random console each month and list its most wanted games increasing the amount of games available to complete the quest. For this month the most wanted PC-8000/8800 games will be featured. These games will only be eligible for DevQuest 007 until the next issue of RANews is released.
