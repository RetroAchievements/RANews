What’s RANews?

RANews is a monthly digital magazine that aims to summarize what is happening/had happened in the RetroAchievements scene over the last month.

How to Contribute

If you have an idea for an article to be posted in the next RANews, please go to the RAdmin profile page and send us a message.

Keep in mind that the article must be written in markdown format. If you don’t know it, you can learn it in 10 minutes following this online tutorial here: https://commonmark.org/help/tutorial/ (it’s pretty similar to what you may be already using on discord).
