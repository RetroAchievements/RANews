Halloween 2023 Event

The Halloween 2023 event is live and waiting for you to master some spooky games. Matering 1, 3, or 7 of the below games will earn you one of the event badges. Bonus challenges are available to take the place of one of the mastery requirements. Check out the official forum topic for more information.
